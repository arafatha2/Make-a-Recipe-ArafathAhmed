Waffles

Waffles are a simple and very traditional breakfast meal for many people. It's been around for a very long time and is a very common food to eat for breakfast. People have made different kinds of waffles around the world which gives it a unique taste depending on where you eat it.

Image from Flickr

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Ingredients from allrecipies.com

Method

How to make The Waffles

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.
  3. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  4. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  5. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.
